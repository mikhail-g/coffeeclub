## Evaluation Criteria

A specialty cafe should:
- Know what coffee they're serving (origin, roaster, processing)
- Use calibrated equipment (espresso machine dialed in, proper grinder)
- Offer filter options (V60, AeroPress, batch brew, etc.)
- Have staff who can answer questions about the coffee
- Source from a genuine specialty roaster (see checklist)

Common flaws to note:
- Serving specialty beans through a poorly calibrated espresso setup
- Dark-roasting beans sourced from specialty farms (loses the point)
- Specialty vocabulary without specialty knowledge behind it

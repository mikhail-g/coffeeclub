# Roasters in Malaga

---

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | What to look for |
|---|---|
| **Freshness** | Roast date on bag (not best-before); beans have aroma when opened |
| **Transparency** | SCA score, origin (farm/region), processing method, variety |
| **Brew guidance** | Roast profile or brewer recommendation labeled (filter / espresso / omni) |
| **Taste notes** | Grounded in origin/processing, not invented marketing |
| **Price** | Reasonable for genuine specialty (~€15–20/250g); not inflated for rebranding |
| **Range** | Multiple single origins available; decaf option present |
| **Availability** | Fresh stock buyable locally or ships to Malaga quickly |

See [Spotting Fake Specialty](../concepts/spotting-fake-specialty.md) for the full checklist.

# Roasters in Canary Islands

---

## Why Canary Islands?

The Canary Islands have developed a strong specialty coffee scene, particularly in Tenerife and Gran Canaria. They ship within Spain with reasonable transit times and have produced some notable roasters with genuine specialty credentials.

An added dimension: the Canary Islands grow their own coffee (primarily in La Palma, La Gomera, and Tenerife) — a rare opportunity to try Spanish-grown specialty coffee.

## Canary Islands Own-Grown Coffee

- **La Palma** — main coffee-growing island; small lots, volcanic soil, high altitude
- **La Gomera** — very limited production
- **Tenerife** — some farms in the Anaga and Teno regions

Canarian coffee is rarely scored publicly but the best lots are genuinely specialty-grade and worth trying for curiosity and provenance.

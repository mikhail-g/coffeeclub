# Roasters in Spain (ships to Malaga)

## Notes

Focused on roasters that:
- Ship to Malaga in reasonable time (1–3 days)
- Offer genuine specialty (traceable, scored, or transparently sourced)
- Carry single origins suitable for filter brewing

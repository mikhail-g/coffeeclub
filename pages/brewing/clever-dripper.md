# Brewing: Clever Dripper

---

## Equipment

- Clever Dripper (size S or L — L recommended for single cups 300–500ml)
- Paper filters (Clever-specific or Melitta #4)
- Manual grinder
- Scale
- Kettle
- Timer
- Filtered or suitable bottled water (see [water guide](water.md))

---

## Why Clever Dripper

The Clever is a full-immersion brewer with a valve — coffee steeps like a French press, then drains through a paper filter. This gives:
- More body than a V60
- Cleaner cup than a French press (no fines)
- More forgiving than a V60 (no pour technique required)
- Consistent results with minimal variables

Ideal for tasting a coffee without technique interference.

---

## Key Variables

| Variable | Typical range |
|---|---|
| Dose | 20–30g (L size) |
| Water | 300–450ml |
| Ratio | 1:15 to 1:17 |
| Temperature | 90–96°C |
| Grind | Medium (coarser than AeroPress, similar to V60) |
| Steep time | 3:00–4:00 |

## Clever vs AeroPress

| | Clever Dripper | AeroPress |
|---|---|---|
| Body | Medium-high | Medium-high |
| Clarity | High (paper) | High (paper) |
| Control | Low (forgiving) | High (many variables) |
| Best for | Tasting / consistency | Experimentation |
| Portability | Low | High |

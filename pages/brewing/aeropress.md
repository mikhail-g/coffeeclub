# Brewing: AeroPress

---

## Equipment

- AeroPress (original or Go)
- Manual grinder (medium-fine to medium, adjust per recipe)
- Scale (0.1g precision preferred)
- Kettle (gooseneck recommended for control)
- Timer
- Filtered or suitable bottled water (see [water guide](water.md))

---

## Key Variables

| Variable | Typical range |
|---|---|
| Dose | 15–18g |
| Water | 200–250ml |
| Ratio | 1:13 to 1:16 |
| Temperature | 80–96°C (lower for light roasts can work; experiment) |
| Grind | Medium-fine (finer than filter, coarser than espresso) |
| Brew time | 1:30–3:00 total |

## AeroPress World Championship Context

The World AeroPress Championship (WAC) runs annually. Published winning recipes are a useful source of inspiration — they are often unconventional (very fine grind, high temperature, short steep) and worth adapting.
